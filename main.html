ABOUT US

The GMETAL has increased its sales/distribution network with the creation of several productive and commercial subsidiaries in strategic geographical areas around the world which support the distribution and production of ukranian mill.

Our metal is used to produce:

  • parts for vehicles and machinery
  • seamless tubes and bearings
  • tools for further processing of metals.

The quality management system GMETAL is certified for compliance with the international standard ISO 9001: 2015 by the certification body TÜV Thüringen eV (Germany), the official representative of which in Ukraine is TÜV.
